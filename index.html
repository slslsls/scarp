Hello World

I'm a full-stack web developer with experience in JavaScript, NodeJS, AngularJS, CSS, C#, and SQL. I love learning new concepts and solving problems in order to build things as part of a team.

About Me

I started coding in 2015 and was immediately hooked. I really enjoy the combination of creativity and logic needed to solve problems and build things in the world of software and web development. In February 2016 I began a six-month coding bootcamp, focusing on full-stack development with mostly JavaScript-based technologies like Node.js and Angular. It was extremely challenging, a lot of fun, and served as a confirmation that this is what I want to spend my time doing. Near the end of the program I was accepted into Microsoft's LEAP internship program, which exposed me to the .NET Framework, Visual Studio, and backend data-processing on a large scale. Since then I've been taking online courses to improve my skills, and working on personal projects for fun.

Outside of programming I have a wide range of interests including music composition, economics, astronomy, and linguistics. I recently started volunteering for a non-profit called Kilowatts for Humanity, which works with developing communities to plan and implement off-grid electrical solutions. I'll be working with their dev team to plan and write software for Raspberry Pi data loggers in the field, and to maintain and improve their website.
